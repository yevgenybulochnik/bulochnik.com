Programming

My interest in programming started during my last year in college while on rotations for a diabetic management clinic. The clinic kept track of patients and their related glucose levels with paper and folders. Finding this method of tracking inefficient, I developed an excel spreadsheet using VBA which allowed us to quickly navigate between patient demographics, patient contact notes,and patient lab values, while also providing us insight into our own clinic outcomes. This began my journey into computer science.
